Expense Tracker CLI is a lightweight, blazing-fast, and deeply intuitive command-line application designed to help you take absolute control of your personal finances right from your terminal. Built entirely with Python's standard library, this tool completely eliminates the need for bulky external dependencies, cloud subscriptions, or complex graphical interfaces. Whether you are a developer who practically lives in the terminal, or just a minimalist seeking a frictionless way to monitor your daily spending, this tracker fits perfectly into your workflow.  With privacy at its core, the application securely stores all your financial data entirely locally on your machine via a clean, readable JSON file. It empowers you with a comprehensive suite of management tools: you can effortlessly add, update, list, and delete expenses while organizing them into custom categories. Beyond simple logging, the app features a smart, built-in budgeting system. You can set specific monthly budget limits and receive real-time, automated warnings if a new transaction pushes you dangerously close to or over your financial boundaries.  When it comes time to review your financial health, the tool generates instant, detailed summaries. You can break down your spending habits globally, filter them by specific months, or isolate them by category to pinpoint exactly where your money is going. If you ever need to perform deep-dive analytics or share your records with an accountant, the app includes a seamless, one-command export function that instantly ports your entire expense history into a universally compatible CSV file. Fast, flexible, and completely open-source, the Expense Tracker CLI puts you confidently in the driver's seat of your personal economy!
